📖 術語表📖 Glossary
平台核心術語定義，幫助客服精確理解並向用戶解釋產品概念。Core platform terminology to help the CS team explain concepts accurately to users.
Available Margin
可用保證金Available Margin
總資產中，可用於開新倉或支付費用、虧損的部分。The portion of your total equity that can be used to open new positions or cover fees and losses.
Cross Margin
全倉保證金Cross Margin
帳戶中所有可用資金共享作為倉位保證金，有助於避免倉位被提前清算。All available account funds are shared as margin across positions, helping to avoid early liquidation.
Funding Rate
資金費率Funding Rate
定期支付的費用，用於平衡多空倉位。偏重的一方每小時向另一方支付。Periodic payment to balance long and short positions. The heavier side pays the other side hourly.
Fund Password
資金密碼Fund Password
保護資產操作的獨立密碼，8-16位，含大小寫字母、數字和特殊字符。A separate password protecting asset operations. 8–16 chars including upper/lower case, numbers, and special characters.
Earn
賺取 / 活期理財Flexible Savings
現貨帳戶閒置資產按動態年化收益率賺取每日利息，無鎖定期。Deposit idle spot account assets to earn daily interest at a dynamic APY. No lock-up period.
Gap
跳空Price Gap
資產在一個交易週期內出現的價格空白，通常由重大新聞或事件引起。A price range with no trading activity in a session, usually caused by major news or events.
Impact Cost
影響成本Impact Cost
訂單規模對流動性的衝擊。訂單越大，影響成本越高，下單前展示預估數字。The price impact of an order on liquidity. Larger orders = higher impact. Shown before submission.
Liquidation
清算Liquidation
當保證金率低於維持保證金率（MMR）時，系統自動平倉。基於標記價，非單筆成交價。When margin ratio drops below MMR, the system auto-closes positions. Based on Mark Price, not last traded price.
Mark Price
標記價Mark Price
平台公允價格基準，由多源預言機與內部數據加權生成，用於盈虧計算、清算和風控。The platform's fair price reference, aggregated from multiple oracles and internal data. Used for PnL, liquidation, and risk control.
MMR
維持保證金率Maintenance Margin Ratio
維持倉位所需的最低保證金比例。低於此值觸發強制平倉。Minimum margin ratio required to keep a position open. Falling below this triggers forced liquidation.
Oracle
預言機Oracle
外部數據提供者，提供獨立驗證的現貨參考價格。MC Markets 使用 Chainlink 和 Pyth。External data providers supplying independently verified spot reference prices. MC Markets uses Chainlink and Pyth.
PnL
盈虧Profit and Loss
Unrealized PnL：當前開倉的浮動盈虧。Realized PnL：已平倉交易的最終淨盈虧。Unrealized PnL: floating P&L on open positions. Realized PnL: final net P&L on closed trades.
Proof of Reserves
儲備金證明Proof of Reserves
平台基於默克爾樹部署在 Arbitrum 上的 1:1 儲備校驗機制，用戶可自行驗證。On-chain 1:1 reserve verification using Merkle tree, deployed on Arbitrum. User-verifiable at any time.
Swap
閃兌Instant Swap
帳戶內即時資產轉換，由平台內部定價引擎驅動，無需鏈上確認。Instant in-account asset conversion powered by the platform's internal pricing engine. No on-chain confirmation needed.
Skew
淨偏倚Net Skew
衡量平台多空倉位不平衡程度。偏倚過高時，影響資金費率計算。Measures the long/short imbalance on the platform. High skew affects funding rate calculation.
Mid-Price Input
中間價輸入Mid-Price Input
基於最優買賣價中間值生成的智能限價功能，方便快速下限價單。Smart limit order feature that auto-fills the midpoint between the best bid and ask prices.

價格機制Price Mechanism

🔮
預言機價Oracle Price
參考 Chainlink 和 Pyth 預言機的現貨參考價格，多源交叉驗證，抗操縱。References Chainlink and Pyth spot prices. Multi-source cross-validation to prevent manipulation.
⚖️
標記價Mark Price
平台公允價格基準，結合內部數據與外部預言機加權生成。用於盈虧、清算、資金費率計算。Platform's fair price, aggregated from internal data and external oracles. Used for PnL, liquidation, and funding rate calculations.
📋 訂單類型Order Types
了解各訂單類型的觸發機制，幫助用戶選擇適合的下單方式。Understand each order type's trigger mechanism to guide users in choosing the right approach.
⚡
市價單Market Order
以當前市場最佳一口價立即成交。優先速度，適合快速進出場。Executes immediately at the best available quote. Prioritizes speed — suitable for quick entries and exits.
📌
限價單Limit Order
僅在價格觸及設定水平時觸發。分開倉和平倉兩個方向。跳空時，於跳空後的真實穿透價格成交。Only triggers when price reaches the set level. Covers both open and close directions. In a gap, fills at the actual post-gap price.
🛡️
止損單Stop-Loss
當標記價達到設定水平時自動平倉，防止超出預期的虧損。Auto-closes the position when Mark Price hits the set level, preventing losses beyond the threshold.
🎯
止盈單Take-Profit
當標記價達到設定水平時自動平倉，無需盯盤即可鎖定利潤。Auto-closes the position when Mark Price hits the target, locking in profit without manual monitoring.
ℹ️所有觸發訂單均以標記價 Mark Price 為準，有效避免因單一異常成交價導致的錯誤觸發。這也是為什麼最新成交價到了但止損/止盈單沒觸發的原因。All triggered orders use Mark Price as the reference, not the last traded price. This prevents accidental triggers from momentary price spikes — which is why SL/TP may not fire even when the last price reaches the target.

滑點控制Slippage Control

⚙️ 滑點設置Slippage Settings

  • 用戶可在交易面板自行調整最大滑點容忍度，僅影響手動提交的市價單Users can adjust max slippage tolerance in the trade panel. Applies to manually submitted market orders only
  • 成交價超出設定滑點範圍，訂單將被取消If fill price exceeds the slippage setting, the order is cancelled
  • 系統默認：System defaults: 常規平倉 8%，止盈/止損 10%Regular close: 8%, TP/SL: 10%

限價單保護機制Limit Order Protection

🔒 規則Rules

  • 做多：Long: 限價不能低於當前最優賣價Limit price cannot be below the current best ask
  • 做空：Short: 限價不能高於當前最優買價Limit price cannot be above the current best bid
  • 違反規則的訂單將被拒絕Orders violating these rules will be rejected
  • 限價偏離最優價超過 3% 時，系統彈出警告提示A warning pops up if the limit price deviates more than 3% from the best price

📐 初始保證金計算Initial Margin Formula

開倉所需保證金 = Required margin = 倉位大小 × 標記價 ÷ 杠桿position size × mark price ÷ leverage


全倉模式（默認）：帳戶所有可用資金共享作為保證金，最大化資金效率。全倉倉位的未實現盈虧自動作為新倉位的初始保證金。Cross margin mode (default): All available account funds are shared as margin to maximize efficiency. Unrealized PnL on cross positions is automatically used as initial margin for new positions.

存款相關Deposits

我可以使用哪些加密貨幣進行存款？Which cryptocurrencies can I deposit?+
MC Markets 支持使用 BTC、ETH、SOL、USDT 和 USDC 進行充值。MC Markets supports deposits in BTC, ETH, SOL, USDT, and USDC.
存款後餘額沒有立即更新？My balance didn't update after depositing?+
外部充值需要區塊鏈確認時間，請耐心等待。可在區塊鏈瀏覽器查詢 TxHash 追蹤狀態。確認數足夠但仍未到帳，請聯繫 cs@mcmarkets.com 並附上 TxHash。External deposits require blockchain confirmations — please allow a few minutes. You can track the TxHash on a blockchain explorer. If confirmations are sufficient but funds haven't arrived, email cs@mcmarkets.com with your TxHash.
轉帳時弄錯了地址，資金可以找回嗎？I sent to the wrong address — can I recover the funds?+
區塊鏈交易不可逆。轉入錯誤地址的資金無法找回。請在每次轉帳前仔細核對地址，建議先小額測試。Blockchain transactions are irreversible. Funds sent to a wrong address cannot be recovered. Always double-check the address before sending, and consider a small test transfer first.
存款的資金會進入哪個帳戶？Which account do deposits go into?+
存款入帳至現貨帳戶。如需合約交易，再手動劃轉至合約帳戶。Deposits go into the Spot Account. To trade futures, manually transfer to the Futures Account.

提款相關Withdrawals

我可以從哪個帳戶提現資金？Which account can I withdraw from?+
提現只能從現貨帳戶進行。如果資金在合約帳戶，需先劃轉至現貨帳戶。Withdrawals can only be made from the Spot Account. If your funds are in the Futures Account, transfer them to Spot first.
白名單地址會過期嗎？Do whitelist addresses expire?+
會。每個白名單地址都有有效期，過期後自動刪除。Yes. Each whitelisted address has an expiry date and is automatically removed when it expires.

交易相關Trading

交易加密貨幣需要完成 KYC 嗎？Is KYC required to trade crypto?+
不需要。現貨和合約均無需 KYC。RWA 類標的（外匯、黃金、指數等）需完成 L0 基礎認證。No. Both spot and futures crypto trading require no KYC. RWA instruments (forex, gold, indices, etc.) require L0 basic verification.
為什麼我的訂單沒有在期望的價格成交？Why didn't my order fill at my expected price?+
成交價格由一口價（Quote）決定，已計入根據訂單大小動態計算的影響成本（Impact Cost）。您可以在成交回報中查看成交均價與影響成本。Fill prices are based on the Quote, which includes a dynamically calculated Impact Cost based on order size. You can review the fill price and impact cost in the trade confirmation.
最新成交價到了，但止損/止盈單沒有觸發？The last price hit my level but my SL/TP didn't trigger?+
止損/止盈單觸發基於標記價格（Mark Price），非單筆最新成交價（Last Price）。標記價是多源加權平滑生成的公允價格，能有效防止瞬間異常波動導致的錯誤觸發。SL/TP orders trigger based on Mark Price, not the Last Traded Price. Mark Price is a fair aggregated price from multiple sources, which prevents accidental triggers from momentary price spikes.
我的訂單被拒絕了怎麼辦？My order was rejected — what should I check?+
常見原因：餘額不足、滑點設置過小、限價偏離最優價過大（做多時限價不能低於最優賣價，做空時不能高於最優買價）。檢查後重試。問題持續請聯繫 cs@mcmarkets.com 並附上訂單 ID。Common reasons: insufficient balance, slippage set too low, or limit price too far from best price (longs: limit can't be below best ask; shorts: limit can't be above best bid). Check and retry. If the issue persists, contact cs@mcmarkets.com with your Order ID.

金庫 / EarnVault / Earn

金庫和賺取（Earn）有什麼區別？What's the difference between Vault and Earn?+
Earn 是活期理財，支持 5 種資產，無鎖定期，本金不受影響，風險較低。Vault 是為平台提供流動性，僅支持 USDC，鎖定期 4 天，收益可能更高，但本金存在風險。Earn is a flexible savings product supporting 5 assets, no lock-up, and no principal risk — lower risk. Vault provides liquidity to the platform, supports USDC only, has a 4-day lock-up, offers potentially higher yields, but your principal is at risk.
Earn 的利息用什麼幣種發放？What currency is Earn interest paid in?+
利息以存入的原幣種結算和發放。存入 BTC，賺取的利息也是 BTC。頁面上的 USDC 價值僅作估值參考。Interest is paid in the same currency you deposited. Deposit BTC, earn interest in BTC. The USDC value shown on the page is for reference only.
