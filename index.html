📖 術語表📖 Glossary
平臺核心術語定義,幫助客服精確理解並向用戶解釋產品概念。Core platform terminology to help the CS team explain concepts accurately to users.
Available Margin
可用保證金Available Margin
總資產中,可用於開新倉或支付費用、虧損的部分。The portion of your total equity that can be used to open new positions or cover fees and losses.
Cross Margin
全倉保證金Cross Margin
帳戶中所有可用資金共享作為倉位保證金,有助於避免倉位被提前清算。All available account funds are shared as margin across positions, helping to avoid early liquidation.
Funding Rate
資金費率Funding Rate
定期支付的費用,用於平衡多空倉位。偏重的一方每小時向另一方支付。Periodic payment to balance long and short positions. The heavier side pays the other side hourly.
Fund Password
資金密碼Fund Password
保護資產操作的獨立密碼,8-16位,含大小寫字母、數字和特殊字符。A separate password protecting asset operations. 8–16 chars including upper/lower case, numbers, and special characters.
Earn
賺取 / 活期理財Flexible Savings
現貨帳戶閒置資產按動態年化收益率賺取每日利息,無鎖定期。Deposit idle spot account assets to earn daily interest at a dynamic APY. No lock-up period.
Gap
跳空Price Gap
資產在一個交易週期內出現的價格空白,通常由重大新聞或事件引起。A price range with no trading activity in a session, usually caused by major news or events.
Impact Cost
影響成本Impact Cost
訂單規模對流動性的衝擊。訂單越大,影響成本越高,下單前展示預估數字。The price impact of an order on liquidity. Larger orders = higher impact. Shown before submission.
Liquidation
清算Liquidation
當保證金率低於維持保證金率(MMR)時,系統自動平倉。基於標記價,非單筆成交價。When margin ratio drops below MMR, the system auto-closes positions. Based on Mark Price, not last traded price.
Mark Price
標記價Mark Price
平臺公允價格基準,由多源預言機與內部數據加權生成,用於盈虧計算、清算和風控。The platform's fair price reference, aggregated from multiple oracles and internal data. Used for PnL, liquidation, and risk control.
MMR
維持保證金率Maintenance Margin Ratio
維持倉位所需的最低保證金比例。低於此值觸發強制平倉。Minimum margin ratio required to keep a position open. Falling below this triggers forced liquidation.
Oracle
預言機Oracle
外部數據提供者,提供獨立驗證的現貨參考價格。MC Markets 使用 Chainlink 和 Pyth。External data providers supplying independently verified spot reference prices. MC Markets uses Chainlink and Pyth.
PnL
盈虧Profit and Loss
Unrealized PnL:當前開倉的浮動盈虧。Realized PnL:已平倉交易的最終淨盈虧。Unrealized PnL: floating P&L on open positions. Realized PnL: final net P&L on closed trades.
Proof of Reserves
儲備金證明Proof of Reserves
平臺基於默克爾樹部署在 Arbitrum 上的 1:1 儲備校驗機制,用戶可自行驗證。On-chain 1:1 reserve verification using Merkle tree, deployed on Arbitrum. User-verifiable at any time.
Swap
閃兌Instant Swap
帳戶內即時資產轉換,由平臺內部定價引擎驅動,無需鏈上確認。Instant in-account asset conversion powered by the platform's internal pricing engine. No on-chain confirmation needed.
Skew
淨偏倚Net Skew
衡量平臺多空倉位不平衡程度。偏倚過高時,影響資金費率計算。Measures the long/short imbalance on the platform. High skew affects funding rate calculation.
Mid-Price Input
中間價輸入Mid-Price Input
基於最優買賣價中間值生成的智能限價功能,方便快速下限價單。Smart limit order feature that auto-fills the midpoint between the best bid and ask prices.

價格機制Price Mechanism

🔮
預言機價Oracle Price
參考 Chainlink 和 Pyth 預言機的現貨參考價格,多源交叉驗證,抗操縱。References Chainlink and Pyth spot prices. Multi-source cross-validation to prevent manipulation.
⚖️

📋 訂單類型Order Types
瞭解各訂單類型的觸發機制,幫助用戶選擇適合的下單方式。Understand each order type's trigger mechanism to guide users in choosing the right approach.
⚡
市價單Market Order
以當前市場最佳一口價立即成交。優先速度,適合快速進出場。Executes immediately at the best available quote. Prioritizes speed — suitable for quick entries and exits.
📌
限價單Limit Order
僅在價格觸及設定水平時觸發。分開倉和平倉兩個方向。跳空時,於跳空後的真實穿透價格成交。Only triggers when price reaches the set level. Covers both open and close directions. In a gap, fills at the actual post-gap price.
🛡️
止損單Stop-Loss
當標記價達到設定水平時自動平倉,防止超出預期的虧損。Auto-closes the position when Mark Price hits the set level, preventing losses beyond the threshold.
🎯
止盈單Take-Profit
當標記價達到設定水平時自動平倉,無需盯盤即可鎖定利潤。Auto-closes the position when Mark Price hits the target, locking in profit without manual monitoring.
ℹ️所有觸發訂單均以標記價 Mark Price 為準,有效避免因單一異常成交價導致的錯誤觸發。這也是為什麼最新成交價到了但止損/止盈單沒觸發的原因。All triggered orders use Mark Price as the reference, not the last traded price. This prevents accidental triggers from momentary price spikes — which is why SL/TP may not fire even when the last price reaches the target.

2.3 點差(Spread) — 為什麼一開倉就虧了?Spread — Why Am I Losing the Moment I Open?

📢這是用戶投訴最多的問題之一。This is one of the most common complaints.

定義:Definition: 點差 = 賣價(Bid)與買價(Ask)之間的差距Spread = the difference between Bid (sell) and Ask (buy) prices


MC Markets 的點差是浮動的,不是固定的。影響因素:MC Markets has floating, not fixed, spreads. Factors:

  • 市場流動性(流動性好 → 點差小)Market liquidity (high liquidity → tight spread)
  • 市場波動(劇烈波動 → 點差可能擴大)Volatility (high volatility → may widen)
  • 交易時段(休市前後、重大數據發布前後可能擴大)Session (around close/open and major data releases can widen)
  • 品種特性(主流品種點差小,冷門品種點差大)Instrument (majors tight, exotics wider)

為什麼一開倉就虧?Why losing on open?

買入按 Ask 價,賣出按 Bid 價,中間的差就是點差——這是交易的隱性成本。Buy at Ask, sell at Bid — the gap is the spread, an implicit cost.

⚙️ MC Markets 特殊機制:影響成本(Impact Cost)Special: Impact Cost

  • 除點差外,成交價還包含影響成本Beyond spread, fill price also includes impact cost
  • 影響成本 = 訂單大小對市場流動性的衝擊Impact cost = your order's effect on liquidity
  • 訂單越大,影響成本越高Larger orders → higher impact cost
  • 系統下單前展示預估的影響成本System shows estimated impact before submission
  • 報價公式:Quote formula: 買單報價 = MarkAsk + ImpactPrice(N)Buy quote = MarkAsk + ImpactPrice(N)

CS 必答:must answer:

  • 「為什麼我剛買就虧了?」→ 點差 + 影響成本"Losing right after buying?" → Spread + impact cost
  • 「你們的點差是多少?」→ 浮動,以平臺實時顯示為準"What's your spread?" → Floating, refer to live display
  • 「為什麼現在點差這麼大?」→ 流動性薄弱時段或波動大時"Why so wide right now?" → Thin liquidity or high volatility
  • 「影響成本是什麼?」→ 訂單大小對市場的衝擊成本"What is impact cost?" → Cost from your order's market impact

第三章 · 槓桿與保證金(用戶問題最多)Chapter 3 · Leverage & Margin (Most-Asked)

3.1 槓桿的本質Nature of Leverage

槓桿 = 用少量資金控制更大價值的倉位Leverage = control larger position value with smaller capital

  • 50× 槓桿 = 用 $200 控制 $10,000 倉位50× = use $200 to control a $10,000 position
  • 槓桿放大盈利,同時放大虧損Amplifies both gains AND losses
  • 50× 下,價格反向變動 2% = 虧損 100% 本金At 50×, a 2% adverse move = 100% loss of margin
🚫 CS 不能說的話:prohibited:
  • ❌ 「槓桿越高賺得越多」→ 誘導"Higher leverage = more profit" → inducement
  • ❌ 「建議您用 50× 槓桿」→ 投資建議"I recommend 50×" → investment advice
  • ✅ 「槓桿會同時放大盈利和虧損,請根據您自身的風險承受能力選擇」"Leverage amplifies both — please choose based on your own risk tolerance"

3.4 保證金率(Margin Ratio)Margin Ratio

📐 公式Formula

保證金率 = 帳戶權益 ÷ 倉位名義價值 × 100%Margin Ratio = Account Equity ÷ Position Notional × 100%

  • 帳戶權益 = 帳戶餘額 + 未實現盈虧Account Equity = Balance + Unrealized PnL
  • 保證金率越低 = 風險越高Lower margin ratio = higher risk
  • 全倉模式下,所有倉位共享保證金In Cross mode, all positions share margin
🔴MC Markets 清算線:保證金率低於 50% 觸發強制平倉。MC Markets liquidation line: Margin Ratio below 50% triggers forced liquidation.

3.5 爆倉 / 強制平倉(Liquidation)Liquidation

觸發條件:Trigger: 保證金率低於清算線時,系統自動強制平倉When Margin Ratio falls below the liquidation line, system auto-liquidates


  • 清算基於標記價(Mark Price),不是最新成交價(Last Price)Liquidation based on Mark Price, not Last Price
  • 這個設計是為了防止異常價格或惡意操縱導致錯誤清算Designed to prevent wrongful liquidation from anomalies or manipulation
  • 全倉模式下,一個倉位虧損會消耗整個帳戶的可用資金In Cross mode, one position's loss consumes the whole account's available funds

🧮 爆倉價估算邏輯Liquidation Price Estimation Logic

CS 需理解原理,不需精確計算(以平臺顯示為準)CS should understand logic, not compute precisely (refer to platform display)

  1. 帳戶總資金 − 初始保證金 = 可承受最大虧損Total funds − Initial margin = Max loss tolerance
  2. 最大虧損 ÷ 倉位大小 = 價格可反向移動空間Max loss ÷ Position size = price movement room
  3. 開倉價 ± 這個空間 = 大致爆倉價Open price ± room = approx. liquidation price

示例:Example:

帳戶 $1,000,50× 買入 0.1 BTC @ $60,000Account $1,000, open 0.1 BTC @ $60,000 with 50×

  • 初始保證金 = $120Initial margin = $120
  • 可承受最大虧損 ≈ $1,000 × (1 − 50%) = $500(保證金率降至 50% 時觸發)Max loss ≈ $1,000 × (1 − 50%) = $500 (triggered at 50% MR)
  • 價格反向空間 = $500 ÷ 0.1 = $5,000Price room = $500 ÷ 0.1 = $5,000
  • 大致爆倉價 ≈ $60,000 − $5,000 = Approx. liquidation ≈ $60,000 − $5,000 = $55,000

(實際爆倉價會受資金費率、手續費等影響,以平臺顯示為準Actual liquidation price affected by funding rate, fees — refer to platform display)

🛡️ 如何避免被強平How to Avoid Liquidation

  1. 隨時關注交易界面上的保證金率Monitor Margin Ratio on the trading page
  2. 保證金率降低時及時追加保證金或減倉Add margin or reduce position when MR drops
  3. 提前設好止損Set stop-loss in advance
  4. 不要使用過高的槓桿Don't use excessive leverage

CS 必答:must answer:

  • 「我怎麼被強平了?」→ 保證金率低於清算線"Why was I liquidated?" → Margin Ratio fell below the liquidation line
  • 「為什麼強平價和我算的不一樣?」→ 資金費率/手續費會影響,全倉下其他倉位也會影響"Why is the liquidation price different from my calc?" → Funding rate, fees, and (in Cross) other positions affect it
  • 「強平之後我的錢還有嗎?」→ 強平清掉倉位,剩餘資金仍在帳戶中"Money left after liquidation?" → Position is closed; remaining funds (if any) stay in account
  • 「能追加保證金防止爆倉嗎?」→ 可以,向合約帳戶轉入更多資金"Can I add margin?" → Yes, transfer more funds to the Perps account

5.2 資金費率(Funding Rate)— 永續合約特有Funding Rate — Unique to Perps

資金費率邏輯:Funding Rate Logic:

  • 市場多空失衡時,偏重的一方向另一方支付When skewed, the heavy side pays the other
  • 多頭佔優(多 > 空)→ 多頭付給空頭Longs heavier → Longs pay shorts

  • 費率可以是正數(您付錢)也可以是負數(您收錢)Rate can be positive (you pay) or negative (you receive)

6.1 未實現盈虧(Unrealized PnL)Unrealized PnL

未實現盈虧 = (當前標記價 − 開倉均價) × 倉位大小Unrealized PnL = (Current Mark − Avg Entry) × Position Size

  • 做多:Long: 標記價漲 = 賺,跌 = 虧Mark up = profit, down = loss
  • 做空:Short: 標記價跌 = 賺,漲 = 虧Mark down = profit, up = loss

🧮 練習Exercises

練習 2:做空黃金Ex 2: Short Gold

練習 3:做多 EURUSDEx 3: Long EURUSD

6.2 已實現盈虧(Realized PnL)Realized PnL

  • 平倉後,未實現盈虧轉為已實現盈虧On close, Unrealized → Realized
  • 同時扣除手續費和資金費率Fees and funding rate deducted at the same time

💡用戶常說「我算出來該賺 $100 但只看到 $85」,差的就是手續費 + 資金費率。Common: "I calculated $100 but only got $85" — the difference is fees + funding rate.

6.3 總帳戶價值Total Account Value

總帳戶價值 = 存入資產 USD + 未實現盈虧 + 已實現盈虧 + 資金費率收入 − 手續費Total = Deposits USD + Unrealized PnL + Realized PnL + Funding Income − Fees
